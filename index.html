Leheng Li


Bio: I am a first-year Ph.D. student at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou), supervised by Prof. Ying-Cong Chen. My research interests are computer vision and autonomous driving. I received B.Sc in Mathematics from Dalian University of Technology in 2022. Previously, I was fortunate to have interned at MEGVII Technology.
